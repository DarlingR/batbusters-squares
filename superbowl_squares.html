How It Works

  1. Pick your squares: Choose any available square(s) on the grid for $20 each.
  2. Write your name: Your name will be entered in the square(s) you purchase.
  3. Numbers are assigned: After all squares are sold, numbers 0-9 are randomly assigned to each row and column.
  4. Match the score: At the end of each quarter, look at the last digit of each team's score. Find where those numbers intersect on the grid.
  5. Win prizes: If your square matches the score, you win that quarter's prize!

Example: If the score is AFC 17, NFC 24 at halftime, the square at column 7, row 4 wins $200!
